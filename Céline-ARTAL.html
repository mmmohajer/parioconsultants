Céline ARTAL

Céline is the International Consultant and senior Research Fellow at Institute for Research and Innovation in Sustainability (IRIS), York University, Toronto, Ontario. She offers extensive institutional and field consultancy experiences in Europe, North America and Southeast Asia with partners including the Asian Development Bank Institute, the United Nations and various international business schools in North America and Europe. Celine’s experiences include development of numerous educational and communication tools, risk assessments, organization of international events, and the creation of action-oriented partnerships. She also acts as an intermediary between corporate actors and stakeholders, including government officials and civil society..

Céline holds Masters Degree in International Affairs and Human Rights from Josef Korbel School of International Studies, University of Denver and Masters in International relations from Université Paul Valéry, Montpellier, France.
