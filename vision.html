VISION

The technological revolution over the last few decades has turned businesses to champions of change, freedom and prosperity. This new business stance is increasingly attracting young minds with new ideas to this ever changing and evolving game. Yet, they lack business expertise and resources to succeed. On the other hand, many executives from established businesses are frustrated with the speed in which new technologies have emerged and the extent that they have penetrated every business function. Yet these technologies are evolving, adding to complexity and uncertainty, while offering opportunities. Pario is created in response to this volatile climate.

Its mission is to bridge business and technology.
