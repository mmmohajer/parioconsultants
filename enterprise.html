ENTERPRISE Selection

At Pario, we are very selective in our choice of portfolio companies and take compensation for our work partially in equity in the portfolio company. Thus we succeed when our portfolio company succeeds. The following factors are considered in evaluating potential portfolio companies:

  • Mission, vision, and purpose
  • Soundness of technology and its application
  • Potential for intellectual property protection
  • Talent, enthusiasm, and experience
  • Sales and market growth potential
  • Relevance to our infrastructure and portfolio
  • Potential for high return on investment
  • Manageable risk
